CAREER SUMMARY

A few highlights of my career thus far would be the opportunity that I had of covering the NBA draft live in the "Orlando Magic War Room" at the Amway Arena. Another professional experience that was a highlight of mine was working on "SportsCenter", "Mike & Mike in the Morning", and "First Take" at the ESPN Headquarters in Bristol, Connecticut. I've also certainly enjoyed my time spent covering a plethora of amateur and professional sports at the ESPN Wide World of Sports at Disney, as well as hosting the "Friday Night Football Scoreboard Show" on FM 96.5 in Orlando. Working at Channel 9 Eyewitness News was also an absolutely wonderful experience. I look forward to continually bringing creative and engaging sports stories to the public for years to come!
